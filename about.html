WHO WE ARE

We are one of the leading manufacturers and distributors of premium Laundry Soap products in Nigeria.

As a foremost laundry soap manufacturer in Nigeria, BVM Royal Concept Limited is dedicated to providing superior quality laundry solutions for your daily needs. Our dream is to 'Develop a Cleaner, Healthier Nation' by manufacturing a wide range of affordable and superior quality laundry soap products that are suitable for the entire family.

At BVM Royal Concept, we understand that clean clothes are essential for a healthy lifestyle. Our laundry soaps are formulated with the finest ingredients, ensuring effective cleaning while being gentle on fabrics and skin. We take pride in being one of the best laundry soap manufacturers in Nigeria, committed to excellence in every bar we produce.

Our products go through stringent quality control measures, and their efficacy, safety, and purity are all well established. A product leaves our factory gates only when we are 100% satisfied about its quality. It's no surprise therefore, that BVM Royal Concept products are trusted by thousands of households across Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be the leading manufacturer and distributor of premium quality laundry soap products in Nigeria and across Africa, recognized for excellence, innovation, and commitment to improving the health and hygiene of every household.

We envision a future where every Nigerian family has access to affordable, high-quality laundry solutions that not only clean effectively but also contribute to a healthier, more hygienic lifestyle. Through continuous innovation and unwavering dedication to quality, we aim to set the standard for laundry care products in the region.

Our Mission

To develop a cleaner, healthier nation by manufacturing and distributing superior quality laundry soap products that are affordable, effective, and safe for the entire family.

Our mission is driven by our commitment to:

  • Produce premium quality laundry soaps using the finest ingredients and advanced manufacturing processes
  • Ensure affordability without compromising on quality, making our products accessible to all
  • Maintain the highest standards of safety and efficacy in all our products
  • Build strong relationships with distributors and customers across Nigeria
  • Continuously innovate and improve our products through research and development
  • Contribute positively to the communities we serve through corporate social responsibility
